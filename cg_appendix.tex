This appendix lists the linear combinations of charge basis states
$\ket{m_1,\ldots,m_N}$ that form states with definite total isospin $J$,
isospin projection $M$, and permutation group representation $[\kappa]$.
The coefficients follow the Yamanouchi orthogonal representation for $S_N$
and the Condon-Shortley phase convention for $\mathrm{SU}(2)$ lowering.

The index $a$ labels the basis vector within the $S_N$ irrep $[\kappa]$
in the Yamanouchi orthogonal representation. All states are normalized:
$\sum |c|^2 = 1$.

\subsection*{$N=2,\; j=\frac{1}{2}$}

The Hilbert space dimension is $(2\times \frac{1}{2} +1)^{2}=4$.
The Schur-Weyl decomposition is:
\begin{itemize}
  \item $J=1$: $[2]$ (1, $\dim = 1$)
  \item $J=0$: $[1,1]$ (1, $\dim = 1$)
\end{itemize}

\begin{equation*}
\begin{aligned}
\ket{J=1,\,M=1,\,[2],\,a=1} = \; &\ket{\frac{1}{2},\frac{1}{2}}
\end{aligned}
\end{equation*}
\begin{equation*}
\begin{aligned}
\ket{J=1,\,M=0,\,[2],\,a=1} = \; &\frac{1}{\sqrt{2}} \ket{\frac{-1}{2},\frac{1}{2}} + \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}} \ket{\frac{1}{2},\frac{-1}{2}}
\end{aligned}
\end{equation*}
\begin{equation*}
\begin{aligned}
\ket{J=1,\,M=-1,\,[2],\,a=1} = \; &\ket{\frac{-1}{2},\frac{-1}{2}}
\end{aligned}
\end{equation*}
\begin{equation*}
\begin{aligned}
\ket{J=0,\,M=0,\,[1,1],\,a=1} = \; &- \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}} \ket{\frac{-1}{2},\frac{1}{2}} + \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}} \ket{\frac{1}{2},\frac{-1}{2}}
\end{aligned}
\end{equation*}
